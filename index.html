Welcome to the Order

It's a story set in a fantasy world in which the main topics of the story are: Corruption, Pride, and Greed.

This story is aimed towards teenagers and up. There are themes of self-harm, death, and discrimination. If you are used to these themes and are okay with them, then this story will be fine for you. This story only contains swear words on the 'Medium' level of Ofcom's swear words ranking.

The story's main character is a young boy named Corin. He lost his parents at a young age and is now part of a crew of bandits led by an ex-Captain of the Karlam Military.

I hope you have a good time reading this story!

Main Characters

Glow: The power system of the story. It is the manifestation of both the mind and the heart.

Enemies: Throughout the story, the main characters come up against many foes, such as the Karlam Military or other criminals!

Locations: The story takes place on a continent named Trikion. The main countries of Trikion are Karlam, Shenghua, Frenkon and Icium. There is a few other smaller countries and even more cities in each that the story will mention!
