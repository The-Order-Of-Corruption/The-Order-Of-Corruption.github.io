Welcome to the Order

Set in a world where Corruption, Pride, and Greed manifest as physical power. This story follows Corin, a young boy who lost everything and joined a crew of bandits led by an ex-Karlam Military Captain.

Warning: Themes of self-harm, death, and discrimination. Intended for teenagers and up.

Lore & Knowledge

Glow: The manifestation of the mind and heart. It serves as the primary power system in Trikion.

Enemies: From the iron-fisted Karlam Military to cutthroat criminals in the wastes.

Geography: The continent of Trikion is divided into four major nations: Karlam, Shenghua, Frenkon, and Icium.
