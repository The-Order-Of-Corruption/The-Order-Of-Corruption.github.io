The Order of Corruption

Welcome to the Order

It’s a story set in a fantasy world in which the main topics of the story are: Corruption, Pride, and Greed.

This story is aimed towards teenagers and up. There are themes of self-harm, death, and discrimination. If you are used to these themes and are okay with them, then this story will be fine for you. This story only contains swear words on the ‘Medium’ level of Ofcom's swear words ranking.

The story’s main character is a young boy named Corin. He lost his parents at a young age and is now part of a crew of bandits led by an ex-Captain of the Karlam Military.

I hope you have a good time reading this story!

Story Blurb

In a crumbling kingdom ruled by deceit, young warriors rise with the power of Glow. Mikael, with diamond-based powers, and Thorne, his rival of steel, are caught in a deadly spiral of power, war, and betrayal. But as darkness consumes the realm, will they rise as heroes or fall as monsters?

Main Characters

Mikael: A prodigy who controls diamond through Glow. Determined, tactical, but haunted by loss.

Thorne: A steel-wielding warrior with unmatched endurance and a grudge against the order.

World & Glow System

The world is divided by class, Glow power, and hidden agendas. Glow manifests in elemental or material forms—Fire, Wind, Steel, Diamond, Glass, and more. Each wielder is bound by their element’s rules… or breaks them, at a price.
